Korean sentence mining, made fast.

Kaeda turns local Korean video and SRT subtitles into Anki‑ready flashcards. Navigate with your keyboard, select words, and export — all in one desktop app.

Download Kaeda View on GitHub

Why Kaeda?

Keyboard‑driven workflow

Navigate subtitles, select target words, save cards, and mark lines known without lifting your hands from the keyboard.

Smart word suggestions

Built‑in Korean dictionary auto‑fills the target word and explanation. Editable, so you stay in control.

Anki‑ready export

Export your session cards as a TSV file that imports directly into Anki.

Local & private

Everything runs on your machine. No accounts, no cloud uploads. Your mining data stays yours.

Known‑line persistence

Mark subtitles as known and they stay dimmed across sessions. Focus on new material, not déjà vu.

Line‑level audio replay

Replay the current subtitle with one click or hotkey. The video seeks to the right moment and plays just the line.
