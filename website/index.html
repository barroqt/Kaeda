Why Kaeda?

Keyboard‑driven workflow

Navigate subtitles, select target words, save cards, and mark lines known — all without lifting your hands from the keyboard.

Smart word suggestions

Built‑in Korean tokenizer and lemma lookup auto‑fill the target word and explanation. Editable, so you stay in control.

Anki‑ready export

Export your session cards as a TSV file that imports directly into Anki. Sentence, target, explanation, deck, and tags — no mapping hassle.

Local & private

Everything runs on your machine. No accounts, no cloud uploads. Your mining data stays yours.

Known‑line persistence

Mark subtitles as known and they stay dimmed across sessions. Focus on new material, not déjà vu.

Line‑level audio replay

Replay the current subtitle with one click or hotkey. The video seeks to the right moment and plays just the line.
